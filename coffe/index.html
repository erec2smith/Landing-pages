Our Story

Born from a passion for the perfect cup

Erec started as a small corner café in the heart of Manhattan. What began as a dream between two friends who believed coffee could be more than a morning ritual has grown into a beloved sanctuary for those who appreciate the craft.

We source our beans directly from farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building relationships with growers who share our obsession with quality. Every roast is a story. Every cup, an experience.
